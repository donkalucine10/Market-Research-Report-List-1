<p><p>The anhydrous caustic soda market is primarily divided into two main types: caustic soda flakes and caustic soda particles. Caustic soda flakes are solid forms that offer high purity and are easily soluble in water, making them suitable for various industrial applications such as pulp and paper, textiles, and chemical manufacturing. Caustic soda particles, on the other hand, are granular in nature and provide better handling qualities, making them ideal for use in applications requiring controlled dissolution rates, such as in the food industry and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
