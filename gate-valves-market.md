<p><strong>The Gate Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Wedge</li><li>Flexible Wedge</li><li>Split Wedge</li><li>Parallel Disk</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gate valves are crucial components in fluid control systems, with various types catering to specific applications. Solid wedge gate valves offer durability and minimal obstructions, making them ideal for high-pressure environments. Flexible wedge types accommodate thermal expansion, ensuring a tight seal under temperature fluctuations. Split wedge valves enhance sealing performance by allowing slight misalignments, while parallel disk valves provide a uniform flow path, reducing turbulence. Each type serves distinct operational needs, influencing their demand in various industrial markets.</p></p>
