<p><p>The Polyurethane Couplings market is divided into two main types: flexible urethane materials and rigid urethane materials. Flexible urethane materials are designed to absorb shock and vibration, making them ideal for applications that require movement and dampening, such as in machinery and automotive sectors. Rigid urethane materials, on the other hand, provide high strength and durability, suitable for heavy-duty applications where stability and load-bearing capacity are essential, such as in construction and industrial equipment. Both types enhance performance and longevity in their respective applications.</p></p>
