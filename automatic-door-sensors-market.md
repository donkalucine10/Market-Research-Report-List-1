<p><p>The automatic door sensors market primarily comprises two main types: microwave sensors and infrared sensors. Microwave sensors use electromagnetic waves to detect motion and can function effectively through obstacles, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Infrared sensors, on the other hand, detect changes in heat or light, typically activated by human presence. These sensors are widely used in commercial and residential settings, enhancing convenience and energy efficiency by automating door operations and improving accessibility.</p></p>
