<p><strong>The Secondary Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Incremental Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The secondary encoders market encompasses two main types: absolute and incremental encoders. Absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each possible shaft angle, ensuring precise tracking of position even after power loss. In contrast, incremental encoders generate signals based on movement, offering relative position changes but requiring a reference point for absolute positioning. Both types are essential in industrial automation, robotics, and motion control systems, catering to diverse applications that demand accuracy and reliability in position sensing.</p></p>
