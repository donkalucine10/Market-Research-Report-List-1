<p><p>The holographic concave grating market is primarily divided into two main types: transmission gratings and reflective gratings. </p><p>Transmission gratings allow light to pass through while dispersing it into its component wavelengths, making them ideal for applications requiring compactness and low angle dispersion. In contrast, reflective gratings reflect light off their surface, which is suitable for high-resolution spectroscopy and imaging applications. Both types serve vital roles in optical devices, spectroscopy, and telecommunications, catering to diverse technological demands and industries.</p></p>
