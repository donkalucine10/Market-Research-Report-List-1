<p><p>The photoionization detectors (PID) market is divided into two main types: single photoionization detectors and multi photoionization detectors. Single photoionization detectors utilize a single ionization source to detect volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other gases, making them ideal for basic monitoring tasks. In contrast, multi photoionization detectors feature multiple ionization sources, allowing for more comprehensive analysis of various compounds simultaneously, enhancing sensitivity and accuracy. Both types serve critical roles in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and hazardous material detection.</p></p>
