<p><p>The Carbon Black for Tires market primarily comprises two types: Thermal Carbon Black and Furnace Carbon Black. Thermal Carbon Black is produced using a high-temperature process, yielding a product that enhances tire durability and reduces rolling resistance. In contrast, Furnace Carbon Black is manufactured through the combustion of hydrocarbons, offering superior reinforcement and higher tensile strength. Both types play crucial roles in tire manufacturing, impacting performance characteristics such as traction, longevity, and overall safety.</p></p>
