<p><p>The glass and plastic greenhouse market is divided into two primary types: glass greenhouses and plastic greenhouses. Glass greenhouses provide excellent durability and a longer lifespan, offering superior light transmission and insulation. However, they may have higher initial costs and require more structural support. In contrast, plastic greenhouses, typically made from polyethylene or polycarbonate, are lightweight, cost-effective, and easier to install and maintain. They also allow for good light diffusion but may need more frequent replacement due to wear and tear.</p></p>
