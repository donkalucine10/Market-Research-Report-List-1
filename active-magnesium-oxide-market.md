<p><p>The Active Magnesium Oxide market is categorized into three main types based on their activity levels: Active-150, Active-120, and Active-80. Active-150 has the highest activity level, making it ideal for applications requiring strong reactivity, such as in refractory materials. Active-120 balances reactivity and cost-effectiveness, suitable for mid-range applications like fertilizers and animal feed. Active-80 has lower reactivity, often used in construction materials and as a filler, providing a more economical option for less demanding applications.</p></p>
