<p><p>The Automated Valet Parking System (AVPS) market is primarily categorized into two types: Millimeter Wave Radar and Surround View systems. Millimeter Wave Radar utilizes advanced radar technology to detect obstacles and monitor vehicle surroundings, enabling precise maneuvering in tight parking spaces. Surround View systems, on the other hand, use multiple cameras to create a 360-degree view around the vehicle, enhancing spatial awareness and safety during parking. Both technologies aim to streamline the parking process, enhance user convenience, and improve vehicle safety.</p></p>
