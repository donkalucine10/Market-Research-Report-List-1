<p><p>The semiconductor metrology equipment market encompasses various technologies used for precision measurements during semiconductor manufacturing. Optically-based metrology utilizes light to assess critical dimensions and surface properties, offering high-speed and non-destructive analysis. In contrast, electron beam metrology employs a focused beam of electrons for extremely high-resolution measurement, allowing for detailed inspection at the nanoscale. Both techniques are essential for ensuring the performance and reliability of semiconductor devices, each with its own advantages depending on the application's specific requirements.</p></p>
