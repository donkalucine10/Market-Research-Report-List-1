<p><p>The Drilling and Foundation Equipment market encompasses various techniques used for subsurface exploration and construction. Kelly Drilling employs a kelly bar to maintain drilling integrity, ideal for deep foundations. Continuous Flight Auger Drilling uses a helical screw to remove soil, suitable for cohesive soils. Double Rotary Drilling combines rotary and percussion methods, offering versatility in challenging terrains. Other methods include sonic drilling and post-driven piling, each tailored for specific geological conditions and project requirements, ensuring efficient foundation solutions.</p></p>
