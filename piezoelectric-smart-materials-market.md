<p><p>The piezoelectric smart materials market comprises three main types: ceramics, composites, and polymers. Ceramics, such as lead zirconate titanate (PZT), are widely used for their high piezoelectric coefficients and thermal stability. Composites combine different materials to enhance performance and mechanical properties, offering versatility in applications. Polymers, such as polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF), provide flexibility and lightweight characteristics, ideal for wearable technologies and sensors. Together, these materials enable innovative solutions across various industries, including healthcare, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
