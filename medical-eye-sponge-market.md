<p><p>The Medical Eye Sponge market is categorized primarily into two types: PVA (polyvinyl alcohol) type and Cellulose type. PVA sponges are known for their high absorbency, softness, and non-reactive properties, making them suitable for surgical and ophthalmic procedures. Cellulose sponges, derived from natural plant fibers, offer excellent absorbency and biocompatibility, often used in various medical applications. Both types are essential for effective fluid management and maintaining sterility during eye surgeries and other medical procedures.</p></p>
