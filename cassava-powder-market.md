<p><strong>The Cassava Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sweet Cassava Powder</li><li>Bitter Cassava Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cassava powder market is divided into two primary types: sweet cassava powder and bitter cassava powder. Sweet cassava powder is derived from cultivated varieties that contain lower levels of toxic compounds, making it suitable for direct consumption and food products. It is often used in baking, starch production, and as a thickening agent. Conversely, bitter cassava powder comes from wild varieties with higher levels of cyanogenic glycosides, necessitating detoxification processes before use, primarily in traditional dishes and certain industrial applications.</p></p>
