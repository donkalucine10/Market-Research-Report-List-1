<p><p>Activated Carbon Injection (ACI) is a pollution control technology used primarily to reduce mercury emissions from coal-fired power plants and industrial processes. This method is effective, cost-efficient, and increasingly essential as regulatory frameworks tighten around air quality standards.</p><p>The Activated Carbon Injection Market is witnessing significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period.
