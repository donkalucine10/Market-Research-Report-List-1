<p><p>Monocrystalline silicon rods and wafers are essential materials in the semiconductor and photovoltaic industries. The market is primarily divided into two types: CZ (Czochralski) monocrystalline and FZ (Float Zone) monocrystalline. CZ monocrystalline silicon is produced using the Czochralski method, offering a cost-effective solution with good performance for solar cells and electronic devices. FZ monocrystalline silicon, on the other hand, is made using a more precise float zone process, delivering higher purity and enhanced electrical properties, making it ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>
