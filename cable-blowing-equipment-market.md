<p><p>Cable blowing equipment is essential for installing fiber optic and other cables into ducts, leveraging three main types: hydraulically, pneumatically, and electrically powered systems. Hydraulically powered equipment utilizes fluid pressure to push cables, offering high force and efficiency for larger installations. Pneumatic systems employ compressed air, allowing for faster installation and versatility in various environments. Electric cable blowing equipment relies on electric motors, providing a quieter operation and precise control, making it suitable for urban and sensitive areas.</p></p>
