<p><p>The Rare Earth Doped Fiber Laser market includes two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers and Pulsed Fiber Lasers. CW Fiber Lasers produce a constant output of laser light, providing high power and efficiency for applications like cutting and welding. In contrast, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit short bursts of energy, which enhances precision and control, making them ideal for engraving and medical applications. Both types leverage rare earth dopants to improve performance and tailor the wavelength for specific uses.</p></p>
