<p><strong>The Fusion Splicer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Alignment</li><li>Cladding Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fusion splicer market is primarily divided into two types: core alignment and cladding alignment. Core alignment splicers offer high precision by aligning the fiber cores, making them ideal for demanding applications with minimal signal loss, commonly used in long-distance communications. In contrast, cladding alignment splicers align the outer cladding of the fibers, which, while generally less precise, are typically faster and more cost-effective, suited for less critical applications and shorter distances. Both types cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
