<p><ul><li>DC Charging Gun</li><li>AC Charging Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The charging gun market is primarily divided into two types: DC charging guns and AC charging guns. DC charging guns provide direct current, enabling faster charging of electric vehicles by bypassing the vehicle's onboard charger. This results in significantly reduced charging times, making them ideal for commercial and high-traffic settings. In contrast, AC charging guns supply alternating current and are typically used for slower charging in residential and public areas. Each type serves distinct user needs based on charging speed and infrastructure.</p></p>
