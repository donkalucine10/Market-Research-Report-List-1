<p><ul><li>Standard HPLC Pump</li><li>UHPLC Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HPLC pumps market is primarily divided into two types: Standard HPLC pumps and UHPLC pumps. Standard HPLC pumps are designed for routine applications, offering moderate pressure capabilities and reliable performance. They are suitable for traditional laboratory work. In contrast, UHPLC pumps are engineered for ultra-high performance liquid chromatography, allowing for higher throughput, increased pressure resistance, and improved resolution. These pumps cater to advanced applications requiring precision and speed, making them essential for modern analytical methods in various industries.</p></p>
