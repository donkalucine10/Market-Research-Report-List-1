<p><strong>The DNA Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Recombinant Protein Vaccine</li><li>Gene-Based Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA vaccines market comprises two main types: recombinant protein vaccines and gene-based vaccines. Recombinant protein vaccines utilize genetically engineered proteins that mimic pathogen components to induce an immune response. In contrast, gene-based vaccines involve the introduction of DNA encoding antigens directly into the host cells, prompting them to produce these antigens and stimulate immunity. Both types offer advantages like enhanced safety, stability, and the ability to target multiple diseases, driving growth in the vaccine market.</p></p>
