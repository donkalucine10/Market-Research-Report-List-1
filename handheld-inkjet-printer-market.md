<p><p>The handheld inkjet printer market is segmented into two main types: USB and photoelectric interface models. USB handheld inkjet printers connect directly to computers or devices for seamless data transfer, making them ideal for printing barcodes, labels, and quick markings. Photoelectric interface models utilize sensors to detect surfaces for precise printing, enabling automatic alignment and enhanced accuracy. Both types cater to various industries, including logistics, retail, and manufacturing, providing portable solutions for on-the-spot printing needs.</p></p>
