<p><p>The dishwash liquid market can be categorized into two main types: aqueous solutions and organic solvent-based products. These products are typically easy to rinse and safe for various surfaces. On the other hand, organic solvent-based dishwash liquids contain solvents that enhance cleaning power, especially for tough stains, but may pose challenges regarding environmental and health considerations.</p></p>
