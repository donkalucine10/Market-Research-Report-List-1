<p><strong>The Industrial Laser Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-cooled Chiller</li><li>Water-cooled Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial laser chiller market is primarily segmented into air-cooled and water-cooled chillers. Air-cooled chillers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them energy-efficient and easy to install where water resources are limited. In contrast, water-cooled chillers use water to absorb heat, offering higher cooling efficiency and capacity for larger systems but requiring a consistent water supply and more complex infrastructure. Both types cater to the cooling needs of various industrial laser applications, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
