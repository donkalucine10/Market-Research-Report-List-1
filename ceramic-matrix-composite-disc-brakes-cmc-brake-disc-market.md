The market primarily includes two types: C and C-SiC (carbon-silicon carbide). C discs are made of carbon fibers reinforced with a ceramic matrix, providing excellent thermal stability and reduced weight. C-SiC discs integrate silicon carbide, enhancing strength and wear resistance, making them suitable for extreme conditions. Both types enhance braking efficiency while minimizing weight and wear compared to traditional materials.</p></p>
