<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Chamber</li><li>Ducted Fume Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fume chamber market is divided into two primary types: ductless fume chambers and ducted fume chambers. Ductless fume chambers are self-contained units that filter and recirculate air, making them ideal for labs with limited ventilation access. In contrast, ducted fume chambers are connected to external ventilation systems, safely expelling hazardous fumes and vapors outside. Each type serves distinct needs in research and industrial environments, balancing safety, mobility, and infrastructure considerations.</p></p>
