<p><strong>The Nitrogen Cryogenic Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Positive Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen cryogenic pumps market features two main types: centrifugal and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move nitrogen, offering high flow rates and efficiency for low-viscosity fluids. They are ideal for large-scale applications. In contrast, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed volume of nitrogen and forcing it out, making them suitable for high-pressure scenarios and precise flow control. Both types are critical in various industries, including liquefied natural gas and aerospace.</p></p>
