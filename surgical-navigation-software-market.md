<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Electromagnetic (EM)</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical navigation software is categorized into three main types: Optical, Electromagnetic (EM), and Hybrid systems. Optical navigation utilizes camera and light-based technologies to track surgical instruments and their positions with high precision. Electromagnetic systems use magnetic fields to determine the location and orientation of tools, offering flexibility in various environments. Hybrid navigation combines both optical and electromagnetic methods, providing enhanced accuracy and adaptability, making it suitable for complex procedures where precision is critical.</p></p>
