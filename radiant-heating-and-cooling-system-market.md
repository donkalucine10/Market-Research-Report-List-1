<p><ul><li>Embedded Surface Systems</li><li>Thermally Active Building Systems</li><li>Capillary Surface Systems</li><li>Radiant Panels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiant heating and cooling systems utilize surface-based technologies for thermal comfort in buildings. Embedded surface systems integrate heating or cooling elements within structural components, providing uniform temperature control. Thermally active building systems involve incorporating thermal mass in construction to enhance energy efficiency. Capillary surface systems use thin tubes to circulate fluid, regulating temperature with minimal energy use. Radiant panels are standalone units that directly emit or absorb heat through surfaces, offering efficient climate control while improving indoor air quality.</p></p>
