<p><ul><li>Spiral CT</li><li>Conventional CT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 128-slice CT market is categorized into two primary types: spiral CT and conventional CT. Spiral CT, also known as helical CT, offers continuous data acquisition and faster imaging, enhancing patient comfort and scan quality. In contrast, conventional CT involves scanning in discrete steps, which can be slower and may yield lower-resolution images. Both types serve diagnostic purposes, but spiral CT is increasingly preferred for its efficiency and advanced capabilities, driving growth in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
