<p><p>The Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) system market encompasses several key components, including Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), Human Machine Interfaces (HMIs), and Remote Terminal Units (RTUs). PLCs automate processes by executing control commands, while HMIs provide visual representations for operators to monitor and interact with systems. RTUs collect data from field devices and transmit it to the SCADA system for analysis. Together, these elements enhance operational efficiency, streamline communication, and improve decision-making in various industries.</p></p>
