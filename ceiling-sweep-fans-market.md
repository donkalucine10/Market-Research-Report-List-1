AC ceiling fans are traditional models that operate on alternating current, offering reliable performance and a wide range of designs. They often consume more energy and are popular for their affordability. In contrast, DC ceiling fans utilize direct current for operation, providing enhanced energy efficiency, quieter performance, and often more controller settings. This makes them increasingly favored for modern homes focused on energy conservation and advanced technology.</p></p>
