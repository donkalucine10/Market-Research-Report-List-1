<p><strong>The DNA Testing & Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR-Based Diagnostics</li><li>ISH Diagnostics</li><li>NGS DNA Diagnosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DNA Testing & Diagnostics market encompasses various methods for analyzing genetic material. PCR-based diagnostics utilize polymerase chain reaction technology to amplify specific DNA sequences, enabling the detection of pathogens and genetic disorders. In situ hybridization (ISH) diagnostics allow visualization of specific nucleic acid sequences within tissues, useful for cancer diagnosis and research. Next-generation sequencing (NGS) DNA diagnosis offers comprehensive genomic analysis, facilitating personalized medicine and detailed genetic profiling by simultaneously sequencing millions of DNA fragments.</p></p>
