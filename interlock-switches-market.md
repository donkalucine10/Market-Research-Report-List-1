<p><p>The interlock switches market comprises various types, including non-contact interlock switches and tongue interlock switches, among others. Non-contact interlock switches utilize magnetic or optical sensors for activation, providing enhanced safety by eliminating mechanical wear. Tongue interlock switches feature a tongue mechanism that securely locks doors or access points, ensuring safety during operation. Other types may include limit switches, key-operated interlocks, and electronic interlocks, each designed to prevent unauthorized access, enhance operational safety, and comply with industry standards.</p></p>
