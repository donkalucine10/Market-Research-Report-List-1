<p><p>Miniature absolute encoders are precision devices used to track the position of a rotating shaft. The market can be divided into two primary types: single-turn encoders and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders provide position data within a single 360-degree rotation, ideal for applications needing moderate precision without additional turns. In contrast, multi-turn encoders keep track of both single turns and multiple rotations, offering comprehensive positional data suited for complex systems requiring extensive range and accuracy in motion control.</p></p>
