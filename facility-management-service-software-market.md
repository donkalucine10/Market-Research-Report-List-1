<p><p>Facility Management Service Software can be categorized into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises solutions. Cloud-Based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere with internet connectivity. It typically involves a subscription model, reducing upfront costs. Conversely, On-Premises software is installed locally on an organization’s servers, providing greater control over data and customization, but often requiring higher initial investments for hardware and maintenance. Both types serve to improve efficiency in facility management.</p></p>
