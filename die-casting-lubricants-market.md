<p><p>The die casting lubricants market comprises three main types: die lubricants, plunger lubricants, and others. Die lubricants are used to coat the mold surfaces, reducing friction and preventing metal adhesion. Plunger lubricants are specifically designed for application on the plunger assembly, ensuring smooth operation and minimizing wear. Other lubricants include specialized formulations for various die casting processes, enhancing overall efficiency and product quality. These lubricants are essential for improving mold life and ensuring high-quality castings in manufacturing.</p></p>
