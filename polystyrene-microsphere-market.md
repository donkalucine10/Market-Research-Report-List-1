<p><p>The polystyrene microsphere market encompasses two primary types: hollow microspheres and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are lightweight, buoyant spheres often used for applications requiring low density, such as insulation and drug delivery systems. In contrast, solid microspheres offer enhanced durability and strength, making them suitable for applications like coatings and adhesives. Both types are utilized across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, automotive, and construction, due to their versatility and unique properties that cater to specific functional requirements.</p></p>
