<p><p>The Central Inverters market encompasses two primary types: roof-mounted and floor-mounted systems. Roof-mounted central inverters are typically integrated into solar panel installations on residential or commercial rooftops, optimizing space and minimizing land use. In contrast, floor-mounted systems are installed on the ground, often in larger solar farms, allowing for easier access for maintenance and greater scalability. Both types serve to convert DC electricity generated by solar panels into usable AC electricity, supporting renewable energy generation effectively.</p></p>
