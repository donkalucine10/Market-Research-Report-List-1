<p><p>The steam sterilization chemical indicators market includes various types tailored for monitoring sterilization processes. Type 1 process indicators signal that the package has been exposed to the sterilization process. Type 2 specific-use indicators are designed for particular applications. Type 3 single-variable indicators respond to one sterilization parameter, while Type 4 multi-variable indicators assess multiple parameters simultaneously. Type 5 integrating indicators provide a cumulative assessment of sterilization, and Type 6 emulating indicators replicate the sterilization cycle to confirm effectiveness.</p></p>
