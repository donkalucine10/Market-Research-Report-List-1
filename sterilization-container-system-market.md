<p><p>The Sterilization Container System market comprises two primary types: Valve Type and Filter Type. Valve Type containers utilize integrated valves that allow for air removal and steam penetration, enhancing the sterilization process. In contrast, Filter Type containers feature high-efficiency filters to facilitate gas exchange and particulate retention, ensuring optimal sterilization conditions. Both types are essential for maintaining sterility, supporting surgical instrumentation, and minimizing contamination risk in medical environments, catering to the needs of hospitals and surgical centers.</p></p>
